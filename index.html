Select Labels for DLP Policy i Required

Choose which sensitivity labels to include in your DLP enforcement policy. You can use all labels, import from a previous session, or pick specific ones.

Reference

Best Practice Security recommendations

Each card defines one sensitivity label — what it means, the protection baseline it requires, and any regulatory considerations relevant to your context. Cards are filtered to your selected industry and data types. Labels confirmed through the decision flow are annotated with a badge. Use Edit labels to remove any labels you don't need, then export the remaining set as Word or CSV.

Exceptions

Explicit clarifications to surface in the tree

These are known edge cases and boundary conditions for your industry and data types — situations where the default label logic may not apply or where extra care is required. Review them alongside your decision to confirm the recommendation holds, or to identify whether a different label or an escalation path is more appropriate.

Examples

Cross-industry scenarios to keep the tree inclusive

Real-world content scenarios mapped to the label model for your industry and selected data types. Use these to validate your decision — if a scenario matches your situation and points to a different label than the tool recommended, revisit your answers or escalate to your compliance team. Examples update dynamically as you change your context selections.
